Magnus Carlsen wins Magnus Carlsen Invitational

After sixteen days of matches Magnus Carlsen emerged as the champion of the Magnus Carlsen Invitational. Organized by Carlsen and several other chess entities, it featured a $250,000 total prize pool--$70,000 of which went to Carlsen--and invited seven other professional chess players from across the globe.

The contestants played online and broadcasted each game live to a team of commentators on Twitch.tv. During its most watched match (the grand finals between Hikaru Nakamura and Magnus Carlsen) the stream reached 114,000 viewers.
